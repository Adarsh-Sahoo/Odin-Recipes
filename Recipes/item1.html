Kulfi-A Soul-Stirring delicacy

Description

Kulfi is like the love child of ice cream and a warm hug, but it’s got this special charm that makes it stand apart. Imagine rich, full-fat milk slowly simmering away until it’s thick and creamy—almost like a custard but with a deeper, more indulgent feel. Then, you throw in a little saffron for that golden touch and a sprinkle of cardamom to keep it from getting too sweet. The result is this dense, creamy frozen treat that melts slowly, like it’s in no rush to leave you.

Ingridients

  • 1 ¼ cups evaporated milk
  • 1 ¼ cups sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 4 slices white bread, torn into pieces
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine evaporated milk, condensed milk, and whipped topping in a blender and blend in pieces of bread until smooth.
  3. Pour mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish or two plastic ice cube trays, sprinkle with cardamom.
  4. Freeze for 8 hours or overnight.
  5. You can mould it into a popscicle or eat in freeform.

Bon Appetit!
